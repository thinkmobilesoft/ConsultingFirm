About Us

SOAANI PROFESSIONAL SERVICES was incorporated as a consulting firm in the year 2022. Prior to the incorporation, SOAANI professional services has been in business operations since 2011. The Firm consists of three partners who are professionally qualified accountants and HR professional. They are also supported by other qualified and supervisory accountants and trainees who are writing various stages of professional accountancy examinations. The day-to-day running of the Firm is carried out by three partners who are responsible for the various divisions and specializations


In recognition of the pivotal roles of Small and Medium-scale Enterprises as the bedrock of every nation’s economy, we seek to assist them in coping with the challenges of growth by encouraging them to imbibe strategic planning as a culture rather than being bogged down continually by daily trading and operational activities.


SOAANI Professional Services is a Small and Medium Enterprises’ Business Support Service that provides a perfect match for entrepreneurs, balancing the determination to grow with strategic insight that enables small businesses to map out their development over the years of operations. Thereby giving the owner-managers the vision and perspective to understand the direction in which they are taking their business and make informed strategic decisions.


SOAANI Professional Services ensure that business owners have sound financial and management systems in place, giving them not only the freedom but also the foresight to concentrate their energies on driving their business forward.


SOAANI Professional Services competitively priced, creative, and innovative strategic support services are designed to position existing businesses and Startups to be successful, sustainable, and profitable, through the provision of the much-needed managerial and capital sourcing support to visioners, promoters of small and medium scale entrepreneurs. In addition, the Firm’s innovative remediation and business turnaround strategies make it ideally suited to Business.


All these combinations make us the preferred partner for an ambitious business venture promoter, small and medium-scale entrepreneurs, fund investors, high net- worthy individuals who share our vision of value creation and commitment.
