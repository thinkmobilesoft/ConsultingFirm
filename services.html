Our Services

CONSULTANCY

Our approach to consultancy is to ensure that our clients are well brief of the process to effectively manage the expected change that will result from the entire process. We study our client’s needs and proffer solutions that will be in the interest of our client and best practice. While not assuming knowledge of everything, our diverse exposures in different sectors of the economy have positioned us as the needed change agent for our clients. Quite often we are engaged to carry out feasibility studies, business plans, cash flow projections as well as process re-engineering.

AUDIT & ASSURANCE SERVICES

This is one of our main activities, our audit approach comprises of risk-based system audit, and balance sheet audit (both interim and final audits). Our application of a computer-based and value-adding audit approach makes it possible for us to detect errors and fraud associated with internet-based transactions. Our approach goes much beyond the statutory limits by employing an exploratory approach and risk-based techniques that reveal lapses and evaluates performance. We are in the business of providing auditing services and business support to a diverse and discerning clientele. Constant training of our team ensures that we remain abreast of happenings in our field that impact positively on our services to clients. The main features of our systems audit are: Highlighting missing and ineffective controls, and documentation of weakness. Emphasis on areas of financial control and accounting system improvement.
